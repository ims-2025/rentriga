Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026

This page explains what cookies and similar technologies RentRiga uses, what each one does, and how you can change your choices.

What we use

CookiePurposeTypeLifetime
rr_lang Remembers your chosen interface language (EN / LV / RU) Strictly necessary 1 year (localStorage)
rr_consent Stores your cookie-banner choices so we don't ask again Strictly necessary 6 months
rr_favs Saves your favourite listings in your browser Functional 1 year (localStorage)
Plausible _pa Privacy-friendly analytics (no personal data, no cross-site tracking) Analytics — consent required Session only
Google / Meta pixels Measure paid-acquisition campaigns (only loaded after marketing consent) Marketing — consent required Up to 13 months

Changing your choices

You can update your cookie preferences any time from the "Cookie preferences" link in the site footer, or by clearing cookies in your browser. Refusing analytics or marketing cookies does not block any core RentRiga functionality.
